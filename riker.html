Insert Trays

Available Insert Configurations

Mix and match insert trays to build the perfect display for your specific collection.

FT 10a — Watch/Bracelet

10 slots, each 7¼" × 2½". Ideal for watches and wide bracelets.

FT 10 — General

10 sections, each 3½" × 2½". Versatile for many small collectibles.

FT 18 — Medium Slots

18 sections, each 2¼" × 2¼". Great for coins, patches, and small items.

FT 18a — Coin Cards

18 sections, each 7¼" × 2½". For coin cards, earring cards, matchbooks.

FT 20 — Zippo Lighters

20 sections, each 2½" × ⅝". Holds 20 standard Zippo lighters perfectly.

FT 24 — Small Items

24 sections, each 2" × 1½". Compact items, small coins, gems.

FT 32 / FT 36 / FT 50

32, 36, or 50 tiny sections for the smallest collectibles and jewelry pieces.

Ring Pads

72 slots, 14¼" × 7¾". The dedicated solution for ring collections.
