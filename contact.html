Our Return Policy

30-Day Full Refund Guarantee

Product can be returned for 100% credit refund if returned within 30 days of purchase without any obligation. Title and risk of loss does not pass to the customer until the 30-day period expires.

Products returned after 30 days will be charged a 15% restocking fee. All returns must be in original packaging and are subject to inspection. No credit will be issued for product we determine cannot be resold. We pay return shipping only if items were shipped in error.

Any product shortage or shipping error must be reported to our Customer Service Department within 48 hours of receipt. Each return requires a return authorization number — call 1-877-659-2604 to obtain one. Have your invoice and date of purchase ready.
