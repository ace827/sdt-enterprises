Our Story

A Small Business That
Grew Into Three Divisions

S & D & T Enterprises, INC. started as a small family business in the early 1970s in Manchester, Ohio. Over more than five decades, our focus on quality American craftsmanship and honest business practices helped us grow into a three-division company serving collectors, retailers, and specialty shops nationwide.

Today we manufacture and supply wooden display cases, anodized aluminum cases, riker-type chipboards, and lucite acrylic displays — all from our facility in Manchester, Ohio. Everything we make uses the finest quality American materials and leaves our doors individually boxed for safe delivery.

  • Made in the USA — All handcrafted products use nothing but the finest American-sourced materials.
  • More than 50 years of experience — We know display cases better than anyone in the business.
  • Custom sizes available — Need something non-standard? We create made-to-order cases for an additional charge.
  • Stress-free shopping — Individually boxed shipping, a 30-day return policy, and real customer service at every step.
  • Three specialized divisions — Allstate Manufacturing, Ruddles Mills Products, and Blue Grass Cutlery each bring deep expertise.

Materials & Wood Species

Only the Finest
American Woods

Our wooden display cases are crafted from three classic American hardwoods. Each species has distinct grain character and finish qualities — choose the one that best complements your collection.

All cases also available with optional wooden feet (cherry, walnut, or oak) to enable stand-up display. Replacement color velvet inserts sold separately in black, blue, red, and green.

01
Cherry

American black cherry is prized for its warm reddish-brown tone that deepens beautifully with age. Our hand-rubbed satin finish enhances the natural grain and creates an heirloom-quality surface. Cherry is the most popular choice for knife, coin, and collectible displays.

02
Walnut

American black walnut offers a rich chocolate-brown color with dramatic natural grain patterns. It's one of the most sought-after cabinet woods in the world — and at S & D & T, it's available in every case size. Ideal for formal, upscale display settings.

03
Oak

Red oak is a classic American hardwood known for its distinctive open grain and golden tone. Exceptionally durable and resistant to wear, our oak cases age beautifully and are an excellent choice for heavy-use displays or rustic-style presentations.

Custom & Non-Stock Sizes

All three wood species are available in made-to-order sizes for an additional charge. Contact us with your exact dimensions and we'll quote your custom case.
