Lucite Displays & Stands

Crystal-clear acrylic stands, easels, risers, glass domes, sword stands, and display boxes. Perfect for store windows, home display, and retail merchandising.

Product Categories

Everything You Need to Elevate Your Display

Clear acrylic lucite stands bring collectibles to life. From small easels to full sword and rifle stands — we have the right riser for every item.

Easels & Stands
  • Standard acrylic easels (5 sizes: 35A through 40)
  • Double-bend easels — 5 sizes, up to 12" high
  • Label easels — 4½" and 6" heights
  • Sword stands — single and double configurations
  • Hat/helmet stands — 6" and 10"
  • Pistol stand & short/tall rifle stands
Risers & Box Cases
  • Square risers — 2", 4" heights
  • Rectangular risers — R2L, R3S, R4S, R4T styles
  • Glass domes with wood bases — 4 sizes (4½" to 12" high)
  • Lucite box cases with wood bases — 4 sizes
  • Rectangle lucite boxes with wood bases — 5 sizes
  • Marble lucite stand option for glass dome MBL1
Accessories
  • Bannerstone display (model 6203)
  • Available in clear acrylic only
  • Works with all riker and wooden cases
  • Ideal for store windows and retail shelving
  • Home display and office collector setups
  • Contact us for current availability
