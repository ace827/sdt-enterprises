Wood Species

Three Classic American Hardwoods

Choose the wood that best complements your collection. All three species are available in every case size.

Cherry

Warm reddish-brown that deepens beautifully with age. The most popular choice for knife, coin, and collectible displays. Model numbers end in 2.

Walnut

Rich chocolate-brown with dramatic natural grain. One of the most sought-after cabinet woods — ideal for formal, upscale display settings. Model numbers end in 3.

Oak

Classic American hardwood with distinctive open grain and golden tone. Exceptionally durable — an excellent choice for heavy-use displays. Model numbers end in 4.
